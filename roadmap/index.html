The four weeks

One arc.
Four moves.

Each week has a job. The same sequence a good operator or coach would run — compressed into a month.

Week 01
Map

Diagnose before you solve. Ten minutes each to share your business and what you want to walk away with. Then the room names each member's single highest-leverage move and real constraint for the month — not the symptom, the bottleneck.

Week 02
Match

You build something genuinely useful for your paired partner — a teardown, a plan, an intro, a fix. Then the room turns the same lens on you. Structured, specific feedback. No pleasantries.

Week 03
Move

War room week. Bring what you're building and the room tears it apart and sharpens it. Eight operators on one problem at a time. The goal is shipped progress — not updated plans.

Week 04
Measure

Results against what you named in Week 1. What shipped, what didn't, and why. The relationships and the system follow you out — you leave with a partner, a room you know, and a cadence you can run again.
